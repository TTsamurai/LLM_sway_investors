### Republic Services Earnings Call Analysis - Q2 2021

**Date of Call**: July 29, 2021

**Participants**: Jon Vander Ark (CEO), Brian DelGhiaccio (CFO)

### Key Financial Metrics and Performance Highlights

**1. Financial Performance:**
   - **Adjusted Earnings Per Share (EPS)**: Increased by 36% to $1.09.
   - **Adjusted EBITDA Margin**: Expanded by 110 basis points to 30.6%.
   - **Adjusted Free Cash Flow**: Reached $1 billion year-to-date, marking a 38% increase.

**2. Revenue and Operational Highlights:**
   - **Total Core Price**: Recorded at 5.2%, matching the highest level in company history.
   - **Volume Growth**: Increased by 8.1% over the prior year, exceeding expectations.
   - **Recycling Revenue**: Commodity prices increased to $170 per ton, contributing 100 basis points to internal growth.

**3. Strategic Execution:**
   - **Customer Retention**: Maintained high levels with small and large container business at 94% and overall at 95%.
   - **Acquisitions**: Invested $567 million year-to-date; projected to surpass $600 million for the full year.
   - **Shareholder Returns**: Returned $363 million to shareholders through dividends and share repurchases, with an 8% increase in quarterly dividend.

**4. Sustainability Initiatives:**
   - **Operational Greenhouse Gas Emissions**: Reduced by 5% compared to the previous year.
   - **Landfill Gas-to-Energy Projects**: Expanded and converted key facilities, with 15 projects in the pipeline.

### Financial Guidance for Full Year 2021

**1. Adjusted EPS**: Increased guidance to a range of $4.00 to $4.05.
**2. Adjusted Free Cash Flow**: Now expected to be between $1.45 billion and $1.475 billion.

### Innovation and Technology Deployment

- **Technology Investments**: Continuing the rollout of the RISE platform, with 47% of large and small container fleets equipped with tablets for real-time customer notifications.
- **Residential Fleet Technology**: Plans to begin deployment in 2022.

### Challenges and Risks

**1. Environmental Solutions**: Revenue was flat year-over-year; 30% tied to upstream oil and gas sector.

### ESG Achievements

- Recognized in 3BL Media's 100 Best Corporate Citizens list for the second year.
- Supported over 25 charitable initiatives and community projects.

### Summary

Republic Services has shown a robust performance in Q2 2021, demonstrating significant growth in earnings, operational efficiency, and strategic acquisitions. The company is well-positioned to continue this momentum, with increased full-year financial guidance reflecting strong business fundamentals and operational achievements. Sustainability initiatives further align with broader environmental goals, enhancing corporate responsibility and stakeholder value. Future focus areas include expanding technology deployment across all fleet divisions and continuing to enhance shareholder returns.
