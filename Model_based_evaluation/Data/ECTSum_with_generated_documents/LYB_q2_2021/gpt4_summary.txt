**Summary Report on Earnings Conference Call - LyondellBasell**

**Date of Call**: Not specified in the transcript.

**Participants**:
- Bob Patel, Chief Executive Officer
- Michael McMurray, Chief Financial Officer

**Key Financial Highlights:**

1. **Q2 Financial Performance**:
   - **EBITDA**: $3 billion, a significant increase from the $1.6 billion in Q1.
   - Record performance compared to a previous quarterly EBITDA record of $2.2 billion in Q3 2015.

2. **Cash Flow and Liquidity**:
   - Generated $1.9 billion from operating activities in the quarter.
   - Free operating cash flow yield stood at 10.1% for the past 12 months.
   - Increased the quarterly dividend by 7.6% to $1.13 per share.
   - Ended the quarter with $1.5 billion in cash and liquid investments.

3. **Debt and Credit Ratings**:
   - Paid down $1.3 billion of debt in the quarter, reducing total debt by $1.8 billion year-to-date.
   - S&P Global Ratings upgraded the company’s credit rating and maintained a stable outlook.
   - Net interest expense for 2021 to exceed the original guidance of $430 million due to costs associated with accelerated debt repayment.

4. **Segment Performance**:
   - **Olefins and Polyolefins Americas**: Recorded EBITDA of $1.6 billion, with strong demand and improved margins driving an increase of $709 million from Q1.
   - **Advanced Polymer Solutions**: Q2 EBITDA at $129 million, impacted by semiconductor shortages affecting demand in automotive and electronics.

5. **Market Outlook and Strategic Initiatives**:
   - Forecast strong markets for products through 2022 amid high demand and supply chain constraints.
   - Ongoing projects to increase capacity to support growing demand, particularly for Circular Polymers.
   - Strong emphasis on safety, sustainability, and reducing environmental impact in line with global challenges and the Paris Climate Agreement.

6. **Investments and Growth**:
   - Capital expenditures amounted to approximately $430 million during the quarter.
   - Highlighted significant growth investments aimed at increasing LyondellBasell’s mid-cycle EBITDA earnings by $1.5 billion by 2023 compared to 2017.

7. **Safety and Sustainability**:
   - Maintained a total recordable incident rate of 0.22, within the top 10% of the industry.
   - Released a sustainability report addressing key global challenges such as plastic waste management, climate change, and social responsibility.

**Summary**:
LyondellBasell demonstrated robust financial and operational performance in the second quarter, significantly surpassing previous records with a $3 billion EBITDA. Strong demand recovery post-COVID-19, strategic growth investments, and a disciplined financial strategy have positioned the company to continue achieving strong results. Despite challenges like semiconductor shortages and global supply chain constraints, the outlook remains positive with expected persistent high demand across its diverse business portfolio. The firm's commitment to sustainability, safety, and strategic capital deployment further strengthens its market position and financial health.
