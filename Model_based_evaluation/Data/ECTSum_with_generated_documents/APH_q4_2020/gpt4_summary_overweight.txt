## Amphenol Corporation Earnings Conference Call Summary Report

### Introduction
Amphenol Corporation recently completed its earnings conference call, which featured CFO Craig Lampo and CEO Adam Norwitt. The discourse provided insights into Amphenol's financial performance in the fourth quarter and full year of 2020, revealing strong sales growth and robust operational achievements despite the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic.

### Key Financial Metrics for Q4 and Full Year 2020

#### Sales Performance
- **Q4 2020 Sales:**
  - Reached a record $2.426 billion, up 13% year-over-year in U.S. dollars.
  - Organic growth stood at 11%.

- **Full Year 2020 Sales:**
  - Achieved $8.599 billion, marking a 5% increase from 2019.
  - Organic growth was reported as 2%.

#### Profitability
- **Q4 2020 Diluted Earnings Per Share (EPS):**
  - GAAP diluted EPS rose by 12% to $1.15.
  - Adjusted diluted EPS increased by 15% to $1.13.

- **Full Year 2020 Diluted Earnings Per Share (EPS):**
  - GAAP diluted EPS was $3.91, up 4% compared to 2019.
  - Adjusted diluted EPS remained steady at $3.74.

#### Segment Performance
- **Interconnect Business (96% of sales):**
  - Q4 sales up 14% year-over-year in U.S. dollars.
  - Margins increased to 22.5% in Q4 2020 from 22% in Q4 2019.

- **Cable Business (4% of sales):**
  - Q4 sales down 4% year-over-year in U.S. dollars.
  - Margins were 10.3% in Q4, slightly up from 10% in the same period 2019.

#### Cash Flow and Capital Allocation
- **Operating Cash Flow and Free Cash Flow:**
  - Q4 operating cash flow was $441 million, and free cash flow was $371 million.
  - Full year operating cash flow reached $1.592 billion with free cash flow at $1.328 billion.

- **Stock Repurchase and Dividends:**
  - Repurchased 1.5 million shares in Q4 and a total of 6 million shares in 2020 for $641 million.
  - Announced a 2-for-1 stock split effective March 4, 2021.

### Mergers and Acquisitions Activity
- **Recent Acquisitions:**
  - Acquisition of MTS Systems for strategic expansion in sensors.
  - Positronic and El-Cab acquisitions enhance offerings in harsh environment connectors and interconnect products.
