### Summary Report of Earnings Conference Call

#### Company Overview
The company concluded the year 2020 with robust financial outcomes, surpassing the expectations with double-digit growth across all key financial metrics. This achievement is especially significant given the challenges posed by the pandemic and the strong performance in the fourth quarter of the previous year.

#### Financial Performance Overview
- **Q4 Revenue**: $3,298 million, exceeding guidance by $108 million.
  - Contribution: Over 70% from organic performance and under 30% from favorable foreign exchanges.
  - Growth: 13.9% reported, 12.2% at constant currency.

- **Adjusted EBITDA**: $735 million for Q4, a 14.5% increase, with the entire beat attributed mainly to robust organic revenue performance.
- **Adjusted Diluted Earnings Per Share**: Increased by 21.3% to $2.11 in Q4.
- **Annual Revenue**: Closed at $11,359 million, up by 2.4% reported and 2.3% at constant currency.

#### Segment Performance
- **Technology & Analytics Solutions**: Q4 revenue up 17.4% reported and 15.1% at constant currency. 
- **R&D Solutions**: Q4 revenue increased by 14.5%, with organic growth across both services.
  - Full-year revenue reported at $5,760 million, remaining relatively stable on a year-over-year basis.
- **CSMS**: Declined by 10% reported and 11.9% in constant currency in Q4.

#### Key Achievements and Initiatives
- Launched new tech offerings including OCE Optimizer and HCP Engagement Management.
- Real World business displayed strong performance through leveraging vast clinical data assets for COVID-19 related research and other advances.
- Achieved a substantial increase in contracted R&DS backlog, up 18.5% to $22.6 billion.

#### Financial Guidance and Outlook for 2021
- **Revenue**: Guidance raised by $250 million to $300 million, totalling expected revenue of $12,550 million to $12,900 million.
- **Profit**: Adjusted EBITDA guidance increased by $35 million to $40 million, expecting $2,760 million to $2,840 million.
- **Earnings Per Share**: Adjusted diluted EPS guidance raised to $7.77 to $8.08, signifying growth of 21% to 25.9%.
