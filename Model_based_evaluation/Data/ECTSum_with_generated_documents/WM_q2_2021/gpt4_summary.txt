## Detailed Summary Report on Earnings Conference Call

### Key Financial Highlights:

1. **Operating EBITDA:**
   - Reported an operating EBITDA of $1.31 billion for Q2.
   - Full-year 2021 adjusted operating EBITDA guidance raised to $5 billion - $5.1 billion.

2. **Net Income and EPS Adjustments:**
   - Adjusted for comparability by excluding certain items that management believes do not reflect the companyâ€™s fundamental performance.

3. **Cash Flow:**
   - Strong cash flow from operations of more than $1 billion in Q2.
   - Free cash flow for 2021 is now projected to be between $2.5 billion and $2.6 billion.

4. **Revenue Growth:**
   - Second-quarter revenue demonstrates robust growth driven by price increases and volume recovery, particularly in the commercial collection business.
   - Full-year revenue growth is projected to be between 15.5% to 16%.

5. **Cost Management and Inflation:**
   - Focused disciplined pricing and cost management strategies helped to offset inflationary pressures.
   - Operating expenses improved slightly as a percentage of revenue, from 61.2% to 61.1%.

6. **Capital Expenditures:**
   - Capital spending in Q2 was $396 million; guidance for full-year spending is between $1.78 billion and $1.88 billion.
   - Free cash flow conversion from operating EBITDA stood at 61% in the first half of the year.

7. **Technology Investments and Operational Efficiency:**
   - Continued investment in technology to improve customer engagement and operational efficiency.
   - Leveraging technology for better route optimization and customer account setups, contributing to decreased costs and increased efficiencies.

8. **Debt and Shareholder Returns:**
   - Leverage ratio improved to 2.84 times, with a target of 2.75 times by year-end.
   - Paid $242 million in dividends and allocated $250 million to share repurchases in Q2.
   - Full-year share repurchase expectation increased to the full $1.35 billion authorization.

### Business Segment Overview:

1. **Recycling Business:**
   - Achieved best-ever financial performance in Q2.
   - Investments in recycling technology are reducing costs and improving material sorting efficiency.

2. **Renewable Energy Business:**
   - Strong growth from increased generation and sale of Renewable Identification Numbers (RINs).

3. **Collection and Disposal:**
   - Exceptional EBITDA growth of almost 24%.
   - Volume climbed 9.6% in Q2, surpassing expectations with significant recoveries in commercial, industrial, and landfill sectors.

### Strategic Initiatives and Outlook:

- **Sustainability and Diversity:**
   - New role of Chief Sustainability Officer created to integrate and advance sustainability and ESG efforts.
   - Hosting a supplier diversity event, "Share the Green," to increase participation from diverse suppliers.

- **Pricing Strategy:**
   - Continued focused on disciplined pricing to address cost structure changes.

- **Advanced Disposal Integration:**
   - 45% of ADS operations integrated; aiming to complete migration by year-end.
   - Achieved over $30 million of annual run-rate synergies, with expectations to capture another $50 million in 2022 and 2023.

### Conclusion:

The first half of 2021 has demonstrated strong operational and financial performance across all business segments. The adjustments to the financial outlook reflect the management's confidence in sustained strong performance driven by strategic investments, technology advancements, and robust market demand. Management remains committed to executing on growth strategies while maintaining financial discipline and generating shareholder value.
