### Earnings Summary Report: Q2 Effective Performance Review

#### Company: Advanced Auto Parts

#### Participants:
- Tom Greco, President & CEO
- Jeff Shepherd, EVP & CFO

---

#### Overview:
Advanced Auto Parts has reported a robust financial performance in Q2, showcasing resilience and strategic dexterity amid fluctuating market conditions primarily influenced by the ongoing pandemic. The financial indicators and strategic initiatives highlight substantial growth and operational improvement, supporting an 'overweight' investment recommendation.

#### Key Financial Metrics:
1. **Sales Performance:**
   - **Net Sales**: Increased by 5.9% to $2.6 billion in Q2.
   - **Comparable Store Sales Growth**: 5.8% in Q2, with a two-year stack improvement of 13.3%.

2. **Profitability:**
   - **Adjusted Operating Income Margin**: Grew by 11 basis points to 11.4% in Q2.
   - **Adjusted Gross Profit Margin**: Expanded by 239 basis points to 46.4%.

3. **Earnings Per Share:**
   - **Adjusted Diluted EPS**: Rose by 15.3% to $3.40 from $2.95 in Q2 2020. 

4. **Cash Flow & Liquidity:**
   - **Free Cash Flow**: $646.6 million in the first half, marking a substantial increase from the previous year.
   - **Capital Expenditure**: $58.7 million in Q2, with future projections between $300 million and $350 million for 2021.

5. **Return to Shareholders:**
   - Repurchased 2 million shares at an average price of $197.52 and paid quarterly cash dividends of $1 per share, returning nearly $458 million to shareholders.

#### Strategic Highlights:
1. **Professional vs. DIY Segment**: The professional business saw double-digit growth, outweighing a slight decline in the DIY omnichannel sector but still contributing significantly on a two-year stack basis.

2. **Operational Initiatives**:
   - Focus on strategic sourcing, category management, and expansion of own brands have significantly contributed to gross margin expansion.
   - Investments in technology, such as the new enterprise pricing platform and Warehouse Management System, are set to enhance operational efficiency and customer service.
   - Significant growth driven by the DieHard and Carquest brands, with continuous expansion in strategic product categories.

3. **Market and Consumer Trends**:
   - The recovery in miles driven and used car sales boosts the demand for auto parts.
   - Strategic adaptation to consumer shifts from DIY to professional services post-pandemic lockdowns.

4. **Store Expansion**: Marked by the ongoing conversion and addition of new stores, including 28 net new Carquest stores and a planned addition of 109 Pep Boys locations, despite some delays due to the pandemic.

#### Risks and Mitigations:
- **Supply Chain Disruptions and Inflationary Pressures**: Managed through strategic sourcing and pricing initiatives.
- **Labor Market Challenges**: Addressed by competitive wage adjustments and enhanced employee engagement.
- **Pandemic-related Volatility**: Continual adaptation to safety protocols and adjusting operations in line with health guidelines.

#### Outlook and Guidance for 2021:
- **Net Sales**: Expected to be between $10.6 billion and $10.8 billion.
- **Comparable Store Sales**: Projected growth of 6% to 8%.
- **Operating Income Margin**: Forecasted to be between 9.2% and 9.4%.
- **Free Cash Flow and Share Repurchases**: Revised upwards reflecting strong financial health and commitment to shareholder returns.

#### Conclusion:
Advanced Auto Parts is poised for continued growth with a strong strategic focus, effective management of operational challenges, and robust financial performance.
