### American Water Earnings Conference Call Summary - Q2 2021

#### Financial Highlights
- **Earnings Per Share (EPS):** Increased by 17.5% to $1.14 in Q2 2021 compared to $0.97 in Q2 2020. The six-month earnings for 2021 reached $1.87 per share, marking an increase of $0.22 per share from the previous year.
- **Revenue Increase from Rate Cases:** The company has seen a significant increase in revenues from new rates in effect due to acquisitions and lowered demand in 2020. The regulated operations contributed to an EPS increase of $0.27 for the year-to-date.
- **Increased Investments in Infrastructure:** American Water invested $782 million in the first six months of 2021 focusing on significant infrastructure improvements. They plan to spend $1.9 billion in 2021, with a total of $10.4 billion over the next five years aimed at enhancing distribution and treatment infrastructure.
- **Regulatory Approvals:** Received a favorable order from the Iowa Utilities Board, which approved an increase in annual base revenue of $1 million. Rate cases in West Virginia and California are progressing as expected.
- **Debt Offering:** Successfully completed a $1.1 billion debt offering with historically low coupon rates of 2.3% and 3.25% for 10- and 30-year debts respectively, aiming to support the five-year capital plan and refinance existing higher-rate debt.

#### Operational Highlights
- **Customer Growth:** Added approximately 11,200 customer connections through closed acquisitions and organic growth in 2021 with an additional 86,900 connections expected from pending acquisitions. 
- **Efficiency Improvements:** Ongoing focus on capital and operating efficiencies, with the achievement of a 33.9% O&M efficiency ratio, reduced from 34.3%.
- **Awards and Recognitions:** Several subsidiaries received J.D. Power awards for high customer satisfaction among large water utilities. 

#### Regulatory and Legislative Environment
- **Recent Legislation:** Engaged in legislative measures in New Jersey, enhancing water quality accountability and infrastructure investment, and in Missouri with the new statewide infrastructure surcharge program.
- **COVID-19 Related Regulatory Decisions:** Obtained favorable deferral orders in multiple jurisdictions allowing recovery of pandemic-related costs. Awaiting final orders on pandemic-related financial impacts in Pennsylvania.
  
#### Environmental and Social Responsibility
- **Reduction in Greenhouse Gas Emissions:** Achieved approximately 36% reduction in emissions since 2007, approaching the goal of a 40% reduction by 2025.
- **ESG Initiatives:** Increased visibility of environmental policies and data on the company's website. Recognition in the Disability Equality Index for supporting workforce diversity.

#### Future Outlook
- **Earnings Guidance:** Affirmed the 2021 earnings guidance range of $4.18 to $4.28 per share and a long-term EPS compound annual growth rate of 7% to 10%.
- **Capital Investments and Financial Strategy:** Continue robust investments in infrastructure, supported by favorable financing conditions and a strong regulatory strategy to ensure stable revenue growth.

#### Stakeholder Engagement
- **Customer-Centric Operations:** Continued efforts to maintain high satisfaction rates and effective communication, particularly regarding conservation measures during the California drought.
- **Shareholder Returns:** Delivered a total shareholder return of 126% over the past five years, outperforming peers and broader market indices.

American Water remains positioned for ongoing growth through disciplined investments, stringent operational efficiencies, and a proactive regulatory approach, assuring stakeholders of sustained financial health and industry-leading service delivery.
