**Summary of Campbell's Earnings Conference Call**

**Key Highlights from the First Quarter Fiscal 2021:**

1. **Financial Performance:**
   - **Reported Net Sales**: Increased by 7% to $2.3 billion.
   - **Organic Net Sales**: Grew by 8% reflecting strong consumer demand.
   - **Adjusted EBIT**: Improved by 18% to $463 million, helped by higher sales volumes and lower selling expenses, partially offset by increased marketing costs.
   - **Adjusted Earnings Per Share (EPS)**: Increased by 31% year over year to $1.02, driven by EBIT growth and lower net interest expenses.

2. **Segment Performance:**
   - **Meals & Beverages**: Organic sales rose by 12%; U.S. soup sales surged by 21%.
   - **Snacks**: Organic sales up 4%, driven by notable gains in brands like Late July and Kettle Brand.

3. **Operational Highlights:**
   - Gross margin expanded by 100 basis points to 34.8%, attributed to favorable product mix and higher pricing from reduced promotional spending.
   - Adjusted gross margin affected by cost inflation and operational costs, offset by ongoing supply chain productivity initiatives contributing a 150 basis point improvement.
   - Marketing and selling expenses rose slightly by 1% due to increased promotional activities.

4. **Strategic Initiatives:**
   - Continued focus on driving consumer demand through innovative marketing and product initiatives, particularly in key segments like soup and healthier snack options.
   - Expansion in e-commerce, where in-market dollar consumption grew by 85%.

5. **Consumer Trends and Market Response:**
   - Strong household penetration growth, especially among millennials.
   - High repeat purchase rates, indicating strong consumer loyalty and satisfaction.
   - Effective management of supply chain pressures due to COVID-19, with strategic moderation of promotions to navigate inventory challenges.

6. **Dividends and Shareholder Returns:**
   - Announced a 6% increase in the quarterly dividend, reflecting confidence in long-term growth and solid cash flow generation.

7. **Forward Guidance:**
   - For Q2 fiscal 2021, expects net sales growth of 5% to 7%, with adjusted EBIT growth in line with sales growth.
   - Projected adjusted EPS growth of 12% to 15%, or $0.81 to $0.83 per share.
   - Continued uncertainty due to the ongoing pandemic, though the company's outlook is strengthening as the fiscal year progresses.

**Operational Efficiency and Cost Management:**
   - Achieved $15 million in incremental cost savings for the quarter, part of a broader cost savings target of $850 million by the end of fiscal 2022.
   - Capital expenditures were $74 million, down $24 million from the previous year due to efficient capital management.
