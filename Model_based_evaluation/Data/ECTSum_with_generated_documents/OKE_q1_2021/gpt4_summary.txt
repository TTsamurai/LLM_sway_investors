**Summary Report: Recent Earnings Conference Call - ONEOK**

**Financial Highlights and Metrics: Q1 2021**

1. **Earnings Overview:**
   - **Net Income**: Q1 2021 net income totaled $386 million, an increase from the previous year.
   - **Earnings Per Share**: Increased to $0.86 per share.
   - **Adjusted EBITDA**: $866 million, representing a 24% year-over-year increase and a 17% increase from Q4 2020.
   - **Distributable Cash Flow**: $660 million, up 27% year-over-year and 28% from Q4 2020.

2. **Dividends:**
   - Q1 dividend declared at $0.935 per share, maintained at the same level as the previous quarter.

3. **Capital Structure:**
   - **Net Debt to EBITDA Ratio**: Improved to 3.98 times from 4.6 times at the end of 2020.
   - **Capital Expenditures**: $525 million to $675 million planned for 2021, over 70% decrease from 2020. Key projects include the Bear Creek plant expansion and the Arbuckle II pipeline expansion.

4. **Operational Performance:**
   - Despite challenges such as Winter Storm Uri, the company managed to maintain strong operational performance, with significant contributions from its natural gas pipeline and storage assets.
   - Ethane recovery and increased demand contributed to stronger performance in segments like the Rocky Mountains despite reduced volumes in other regions due to the storm.

5. **Guidance Adjustments:**
   - **2021 Adjusted EBITDA**: Expected to grow over 17% from 2020.
   - **Net Income and EPS Guidance for 2021**: Increased by 10%.
   - **Adjusted EBITDA Guidance Increase**: Raised by 5% from the previous estimates.

6. **Strategic Initiatives:**
   - Continued focus on sustainability, pursuing opportunities in renewable energy and projects aimed at reducing greenhouse gas emissions.
   - Efforts in electrification of assets, potential carbon capture, and storage projects to align with a low-carbon economy.

7. **Market and Industry Outlook:**
   - Positive outlook supported by increasing commodity prices, higher producer activity, and strengthening energy markets. The projected growth aligns with expectations of high single to low double-digit EBITDA growth in 2022 within the $50 to $70 per barrel oil price range.

8. **Challenges:**
   - Operational challenges during Winter Storm Uri, including increased electricity costs and customer facility outages, temporarily impacted volumes.

9. **Sector-Specific Developments:**
    - **Natural Gas Pipeline Segment:** Reliable operations ensured essential services during extreme conditions, recording sales of 5.2 Bcf of natural gas from inventory.
    - **Natural Gas Liquids (NGL) Segment:** Encountering volume impacts but managed through optimization and marketing activities.
    - **Gathering and Processing Segment:** Despite challenges, increased processed volumes in the Rocky Mountain region; expansion plans are on track with expected completion in Q4 2021.

10. **Long-term Strategy:**
    - Focused on maintaining financial stability, innovative transitions to sustainable energy solutions, and ensuring safe and reliable customer service.

This summary encapsulates the pivotal financial performances and strategic initiatives from ONEOK's recent earnings call, reflecting a robust start to 2021 with strong momentum projecting into subsequent quarters.
