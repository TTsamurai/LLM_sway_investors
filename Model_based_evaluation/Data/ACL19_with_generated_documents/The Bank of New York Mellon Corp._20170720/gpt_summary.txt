- Solid revenue growth in both investment management and investment services fees, with a 6% growth in investment management and performance fees and a 4% growth in investment services fees
- Total revenue grew by 5% despite FX pressure due to low volatility
- Net interest revenue and net interest margin benefited from rate increases, increasing by 8%
- Operating margins increased by 2 percentage points to 35%

- Record assets under management of 1.77 trillion, up 6% year-over-year
- Record assets under custody and/or administration of $31.1 trillion, up 5% year-over-year
- Net interest revenue increased to $838 million, up 7% year-over-year
- Adjusted non-interest expenses increased 1% year-over-year and slightly sequentially
- Fully phased-in supplemental leverage ratio increased to 6%
- Effective tax rate expected to be in the range of 25% to 26%
- Expected to generate positive operating leverage for the entire year of 2017

Overall, the company demonstrated strong financial performance in the recent quarter, with growth in key revenue streams, increased operating margins, and improved asset management metrics. The positive outlook for the future earnings reflects the company's well-diversified and resilient business model.
