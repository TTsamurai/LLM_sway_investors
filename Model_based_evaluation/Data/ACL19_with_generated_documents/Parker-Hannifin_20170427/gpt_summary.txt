

Highlights from the earnings conference call transcripts:
- Sales for the third quarter were $3.12 billion, a 10% increase compared with the same quarter a year ago.
- Organic sales increased 6%, acquisitions contributed 5%, while currency was a negative 1%.
- Total orders increased 8% compared with the same quarter last year.
- Net income for the third quarter increased 28% to $238.8 million compared with the same period last year.
- Earnings per share were $1.75 as reported or $2.11 on an adjusted basis, a 40% increase in adjusted earnings per share compared with adjusted earnings in the same quarter last year.
- Overall segment operating margin performance was very strong at 14.8%, as reported, or 16.1% on an adjusted basis.
- Year-to-date cash from operations excluding a discretionary pension contribution was 11.8% of sales.
- The company closed the transaction to acquire CLARCOR and has begun the process of integrating the businesses, aiming to generate $140 million in cost synergies from the combined filtration businesses.
- The company increased its full-year sales growth forecast from a negative 0.5% to an increase of positive 5.9% at the midpoint in the new guidance.
- The full-year adjusted EPS guidance was updated to a range of $7.70 to $8.00 for a midpoint of $7.85.
- The company announced a 5% increase in the quarterly dividend, marking 61 consecutive fiscal years of increased annual dividends paid.
- The company launched a new Voice of the Machine Internet of Things offering, an open, interoperable, and scalable system of connected products and services.

The company is focused on strategic initiatives, including integration of the CLARCOR acquisition, reduction of revenue complexity, and product line simplification to drive margin expansion and achieve long-term financial objectives. The senior leadership expressed confidence in the progress made and the future prospects of the company.
