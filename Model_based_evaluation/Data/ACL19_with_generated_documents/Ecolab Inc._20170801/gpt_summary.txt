Based on the recent earnings conference call transcripts of Ecolab, the key financial metrics for the second quarter of 2017 are as follows:

1. Acquisition adjusted fixed currency sales growth: Sales growth in the Institutional, Industrial, and Other segments increased by 3%, while Energy segment sales rose by 5%.

2. Operating Margins: Reported operating margins increased by 30 basis points, while adjusted fixed currency operating margins decreased by 70 basis points due to the impact of higher delivered product costs.

3. Operating Income: Adjusted fixed currency operating income rose by 1% in the second quarter of 2017.

4. Adjusted Earnings Per Share: The second quarter 2017 adjusted earnings per share were $1.13, representing a 5% increase from the previous year. Ecolab expects full-year adjusted diluted earnings per share in the range of $4.70 to $4.90, with a projected 8% to 12% increase from the previous year.

5. Outlook: Ecolab anticipates improving volume growth and pricing across all business segments in the second half of the year, with a projected strengthening operating income growth and a third quarter adjusted diluted earnings per share in the range of $1.36 to $1.44, up 6% to 13% from the previous year.

Overall, despite a challenging market environment, Ecolab expects to deliver strong adjusted diluted earnings per share growth in 2017.
