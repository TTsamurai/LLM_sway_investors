3. Cash Return to Shareholders - $2 billion through share repurchases and dividends, highlighting the company's commitment to rewarding shareholders.

4. Return on Invested Capital (ROIC) Adjusted - 27.6% on a trailing four-quarter basis, reflecting the company's ability to generate strong returns on the capital invested.

5. Retail Sales Growth - Retail sales of crossovers from Chevrolet, GMC, Buick, and Cadillac brands were up 25% year-over-year, indicating strong consumer demand for the company's products.

6. International Operations Restructuring - The company's strategic decision to combine all operations outside of China and North America under Barry Engle is expected to drive greater efficiencies and support long-term growth opportunities.

8. Future Mobility Initiatives - The company's commitment to an all-electric future and plans to launch at least 20 new all-electric vehicles by 2023, including two in the next 18 months, show a forward-looking approach to align with evolving industry trends.

9.
