**Summary Report: Earnings Conference Call Q1**

**1. Financial Highlights:**
   - **Revenue Growth:**
     - Q1 revenue increased by 8% year-over-year, driven by a 6% increase in recurring subscription revenue and an 18% increase in asset-based fee revenue.
     - Total operating revenues, excluding FX impacts, increased by 9%.
   
   - **Expense and Profitability:**
     - Operating expenses and adjusted EBITDA expenses grew by 3% on a reported basis and 4.8% and 5.3% respectively, excluding FX impacts.
     - Operating income rose by 15%; adjusted EBITDA went up by 13%.
     - Operating margin improved by 280 basis points; adjusted EBITDA margin improved by 220 basis points to 50%.

   - **Earnings Per Share (EPS):**
     - Diluted EPS and adjusted EPS increased by 33% and 29% respectively.

   - **Tax Rate and Cash Flow:**
     - The effective tax rate was 28.2%, down from 33.5% the previous year.
     - Q1 free cash flow was $27.4 million, a decrease of $4 million due to higher expenses and capex, offset partially by higher cash collections.

**2. Segment Performance:**
   - **Index Segment:**
     - Revenue increased by 13%, index asset-based fee revenue up by 18%.
     - Quarter sales were $19 million, an 11% increase.
     - Adjusted EBITDA margin for the segment was 70.8%.

   - **Analytics Segment:**
     - Revenues rose by 2% to $112 million (3.3% increase excluding FX).
     - Run rate grew by $10 million.
     - Adjusted EBITDA margin decreased to 26.3%.

   - **All Other Segment:**
     - Revenue grew by 6% reported and 12% adjusted for disposals and FX.
     - ESG revenue increased by 17% to $13 million.
     - Adjusted EBITDA margin improved significantly to 21.8%.

**3. Capital and Shareholder Returns:**
   - Repurchased and settled 1.1 million shares in Q1.
   - $0.8 billion remaining in share repurchase authorization.
   - Cumulative return of $2.4 billion through repurchases and dividends since 2012.

**4. Balance Sheet and Cash Position:**
   - Cash and cash equivalents stood at $697 million.
   - Leverage ratio was 3.6 times at the end of Q1.

**5. Guidance and Outlook:**
   - Reaffirmed full-year 2017 guidance.
   - Continued focus on growth, efficient infrastructure, and productivity.

**6. Further Observations:**
   - The company has demonstrated consistent growth in its key segments, particularly in its Index product line.
   - It continues to face challenges in the Analytics segment, affected by market conditions and high cancellations in recent quarters.
   - Strategic product and client segment focus, especially in multi-asset class asset managers and banking sector for risk solutions, are expected to drive future sales growth.

**7. Strategic Moves:**
   - Strong discipline in capital deployment and investments targeted towards innovation in product offerings, such as ESG and Index services, which are gaining traction in the market.
