**Akamai Q1 Earnings Summary and Investment Recommendation: Overweight**

**Revenue Highlights:**
- **Total Revenue:** $609 million, up 7% year-over-year (YoY).
- **Performance and Security Solutions Revenue:** $369 million, up 18% YoY, driving overall revenue growth. Represents over 60% of total revenue. 
- **Cloud Security Solutions Revenue:** $110 million, up 37% YoY, signaling strong performance in the security segment.
- **Media Delivery Solutions Revenue:** $187 million, down 9% YoY but up 3% excluding large Internet platform customers.
- **Services and Support Solutions Revenue:** $53 million, up 15% YoY.

**Geographical Breakdown:**
- **International Markets Revenue:** $203 million, up 19% YoY, with significant growth in the Asia Pacific region.
- **U.S. Revenue:** $407 million, up 2% YoY, muted due to large Internet platform customers.

**Financial Performance:**
- **GAAP Gross Margin:** 66%, consistent with the previous year.
- **Non-GAAP Operating Income:** $165 million, up slightly from the previous year.
- **GAAP Net Income:** $81 million or $0.46 per diluted share.
- **Non-GAAP Net Income:** $120 million or $0.69 per diluted share.
- **Adjusted EBITDA:** $241 million, reflecting a strong operational stance with a 39% margin.
- **Cash Flow and Capital Expenditure:** Cash from operations stood strong at $143 million; Capital expenditures were $94 million.

**Operational Insights:**
- The company's focus on high-margin subscription model products in the cloud security arena continues to bolster the financials positively.
- The recent acquisition of SOASTA aims to expand capabilities in digital performance management, expected to further contribute to revenue growth.
- Despite a slowdown in the Media Delivery Solutions segment, management remains optimistic about the long-term growth potential, especially in Over-the-top (OTT) media services.

**Balance Sheet and Shareholder Value:**
- **Liquidity Position:** Strong with approximately $1.6 billion in cash, cash equivalents, and marketable securities.
- **Share Repurchase:** $72 million spent on repurchasing about 1.1 million shares, reflecting confidence in the company's future and commitment to returning value to shareholders.

**Outlook and Strategic Direction:**
- The management emphasizes continued investment in innovation and enterprise offerings, expecting these to drive future top-line and bottom-line growth.
- Q2 revenue is projected between $597 million to $609 million, with anticipated moderate growth in Media deliveries but robust performance in Security and Services expected to continue.
- Long-term expectations include returning to double-digit growth rates by 2018, underpinned by strategic investments and market-leading innovations.

**Recommendation - Overweight Position:**
Given Akamai's robust Q1 financial performance, particularly in high-margin sectors like Cloud Security Solutions, and strategic expansions through acquisitions like SOASTA, it offers a compelling investment opportunity. Despite some short-term volatility in the Media segment, the company's diversified revenue sources, strong international growth, and continuous product evolution position it favourably for future growth. Investors are advised to take an overweight position, anticipating that the strategic initiatives will translate into sustained, long-term profitability and shareholder value enhancement.
