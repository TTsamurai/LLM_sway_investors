Key financial metrics from the recent earnings conference call transcripts include:

- Communication Systems revenue in the quarter was $449 million, up 3% versus the prior year.
- Operating income for the segment was up 22%, resulting in margin expansion of 500 basis points.
- Legacy tactical revenue grew 28% in the quarter and was about flat for the year versus previous guidance of down low-single digits.
- Full year fiscal 2017 segment revenue was $1.75 billion with an operating margin of 29.9%, up 80 basis points versus the prior year.
- Electronic Systems revenue was up 4% for the quarter and the year, with operating income declining 15% due to the transition of the ADS-B program.
- Space and Intelligence Systems revenue was down 4% for the quarter and about flat for the year, with segment operating income up 8% and margin expansion of 120 basis points.
- Fiscal 2018 revenue guidance is in the range of $6.02 billion to $6.14 billion, up 2% to 4% for the year, and EPS in the range of $5.85 to $6.05, up 6% to 9%.
- The company expects to pay down $600 million of debt in fiscal 2018 and generate free cash flow in the range of $850 million to $900 million.

Overall, the company is expecting growth in all segments, with a focus on cost and operational excellence, as well as leveraging synergies between the Harris and Exelis businesses. The company also plans to deploy capital for debt reduction, share repurchases, and dividends.
