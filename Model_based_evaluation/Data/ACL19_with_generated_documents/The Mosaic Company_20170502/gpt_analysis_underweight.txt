- Unplanned mechanical issues at the Faustina Louisiana ammonia plant resulted in downtime and higher ammonia costs, impacting the Phosphates margin rate.

2. Potash Segment:
- Incident with a skip at the Esterhazy K2 mine and Canpotex-related logistics issues led to lower than expected sales volumes and reduced per unit profitability.

3. International Distribution Segment:
- While results were generally in line with guidance, the company expects to issue debt to finance the cash portion of the Vale Fertilizantes acquisition, which will affect the company's financial position.

4. Change in Depreciation Method:
- The company has changed its depreciation method, leading to a non-cash P&L benefit of approximately $70 million in 2017. However, this change may impact the comparability of financial metrics with previous periods.
