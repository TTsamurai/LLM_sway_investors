6. Specialty product offerings: Anthem's efforts to increase penetration rates in specialty product offerings, such as dental and vision, have shown positive results.

7. Individual ACA-compliant business: The company is closely monitoring and analyzing the morbidity of its membership and is assessing market footprint and rate increases necessary to reflect the underlying morbidity and expected cost of this population.

9. Long-term pharmacy strategy: Anthem issued an RFP during the first quarter and expects to receive all responses during the first half of the year. The company is committed to informing the market of its strategy by the end of 2017.

10. Financial outlook: Anthem expects adjusted earnings per share of greater than $11.60 for the full year of 2017, an increase of $0.10 from the previous outlook.
