**Financial Overview - Recent Earnings Conference Call**

**Revenue Analysis:**
- **Total Revenue**: $9.9 billion
    - **Enterprise Services Revenue**: $2.5 billion for two months, not included in discontinued operations
    - **Revenue from Continuing Operations**: $7.4 billion, down 5% adjusted for divestitures and currency
    - **Adjusted Revenue (Excluding Software and Tier 1)**: Up by 1%, reflecting growth in core operations

**Market and Performance Influences:**
- **Challenging Market Conditions**: Competitive pricing, challenging commodities environment, and unfavorable currency impacted revenues.
- **Regional Performance**:
    - Stable U.S. performance excluding Tier 1 servers
    - Decline in Europe driven by weaknesses in the U.K., partially offset by strong performance in Germany
    - Mixed performance in APJ with strong results in Japan and India, but broader softness in the rest of Asia

**Financial Metrics:**
- **Gross Margin**: 33.8%, a decrease of 200 basis points YoY
- **Operating Profit Margin**: Non-GAAP operating profit at 7.8%, down from the previous year
- **EPS**: Non-GAAP diluted net earnings per share of $0.35, within the guided range of $0.33 to $0.37

**Segment Performance:**
- **Enterprise Group (EG)**: Revenue down 7%, but slight growth YoY when adjusted for Tier 1 sales. Notable growth in Aruba (+27%), Three-part all-flash arrays (+33%), and High-performance compute (>40%).
- **Technology Services**: Revenue grew by 3%, reflecting consistent demand and operational stability.
- **Financial Services**: Increased by 11%, supported by lease conversions and a higher mix of operating leases.
- **Software**: Revenue decreased by 9%, mainly due to declines in license and professional services, partially offset by growth in SaaS.

**Operational Challenges and Strategies:**
- Cost pressures particularly due to DRAM pricing and currency headwinds were significant.
- Aim to streamline costs with anticipated savings of $200 million to $300 million in the second half of the year through strict spending control and organizational simplifications.

**Future Outlook:**
- **Q3 FY17**: Non-GAAP diluted net earnings per share expected to be $0.24 to $0.28, and GAAP diluted net earnings per share ranging from negative $0.02 to positive $0.02.
- **FY17 Total**: Maintaining non-GAAP diluted net EPS outlook of $1.46 to $1.56, with GAAP diluted net EPS expected between negative $0.03 to $0.07.

**Strategic Initiatives:**
- Continued focus on organic investments and targeted M&A to fill strategic gaps, emphasizing R&D and integration of recent acquisitions into the portfolio. Efforts are directed towards bolstering core servers and expanding high-margin business areas. 

**Capital Allocation:**
- Dividend payments of $107 million and share repurchases of $670 million conducted during the quarter, aligning with the commitment to return $3 billion to shareholders in FY17.

**Executive Summary:**
Despite a challenging quarter impacted by external factors and heightened competitive pressures, strategic adjustments and focused growth in higher-margin businesses have helped stabilize key financial metrics. The company is actively pursuing cost optimizations and adjusting its operating structure to better align with market conditions and long-term strategic goals. Future outlook remains cautious but optimistic, with substantial efforts directed towards mitigating cost pressures and leveraging growth in technology-intensive sectors.
