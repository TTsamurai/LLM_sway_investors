**Q2 Financial Performance Highlights:**

**1. Revenue Growth:**
- **Total Revenue:** $2.5 billion, marking an increase of $176 million or 7.5% year-over-year.
- **Revenue Breakdown:** Internal growth contributed 7.2%, with acquisitions accounting for a minor 30 basis points, indicating strong organic growth drivers.

**2. Yield and Price Increases:**
- **Average Yield:** Increased notably across the collection business by 2.5%, with the small container business recording a 4.2% yield increase, thereby reinforcing robust pricing power.
- **Core Price:** Overall core price increase was 4.1%, driven by a 5.3% increase in the open market, which signals strong market positioning and the ability to pass through price increases.

**3. Volume Growth:**
- **Overall Volume:** Increased by 1.9% from the previous year, demonstrating demand resilience. Noteworthily, the large container and residential businesses showed volume increases, which are key growth areas.

**4. Margin Analysis:**
- **Adjusted EBITDA Margin:** Remained stable at 28.0%, slightly down from 28.3% a year ago mainly due to increased landfill operating costs, which are expected to abate in the coming quarters.

**5. Cash Flow and Capital Allocation:**
- **Adjusted Free Cash Flow:** Achieved $358 million, up 6% year-over-year. The company reaffirmed its full-year cash flow guidance of $875 million to $900 million, highlighting efficient capital management and the expectation of solid cash generation.

**6. Cost Management:**
- Despite rising fuel prices and operational headwinds such as increased landfill costs, the company has managed to maintain cost discipline, with cost of goods sold in recycled commodities increasing due to higher commodity prices rather than inefficiencies.

**7. Future Outlook and Strategic Positioning:**
- **CapEx:** About 53% of the year's capital expenditures were utilized in H1, supporting the strong volume growth. Management anticipates potential increases in CapEx, suggesting ongoing investments to fuel growth.
- **Margin Expansion:** Despite current pressures, management anticipates margin expansion in the second half of the year as landfill costs normalize, and revenues continue to grow.

**Risks:**
- The main risks include fluctuating recycling commodity prices and potential regulatory changes that could impact operational costs. However, these are mitigated by the company's diversified revenue streams and strong market presence.

**Conclusion and Investment Recommendation:**

Given the company's strong performance in revenue growth, effective cost management, stable margins despite temporary cost pressures, and confident cash flow projections, we maintain an overweight recommendation. We expect the company to continue demonstrating financial resilience and growth, supported by strategic initiatives and robust market demand. Investors should consider increasing their holdings to capitalize on anticipated value growth and solid financial health.
