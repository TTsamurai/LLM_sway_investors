**Promotional Investment Report on Broadridge Financial Solutions, Inc.**

**Executive Summary:**
Broadridge Financial Solutions, Inc. (NYSE: BR) has demonstrated exceptional operational and financial performance in the third quarter of fiscal year 2017. With robust earnings, substantial growth in revenue, and promising strategic acquisitions, we strongly recommend an 'overweight' investment stance on Broadridge.

**Investment Highlights:**

1. **Stellar Financial Growth:**
   - **Revenue:** Total revenues surged by 46% year-over-year to $1.01 billion, driven significantly by the strategic acquisition of NACC, which alone contributed $277 million. This impressive top-line growth speaks volumes about Broadridge’s dominance and expansion strategy in the financial services industry.
   - **Adjusted Earnings Per Share (EPS):** Adjusted EPS grew by a remarkable 19% to $0.69, reflecting the company's ability to enhance profitability amidst significant growth and investment activities.
   - **Recurring Fee Revenue:** This key metric rose 30% to $592 million, highlighting the reliability and strength of Broadridge’s business model. Importantly, excluding acquisitions, organic recurring fee revenue growth stood at 4%.

2. **Strategic Acquisitions:**
   - **Message Automation and NACC:** These acquisitions not only expanded the company's portfolio but also contributed to revenue growth. Particularly, the investment in Message Automation realized a $9 million non-cash gain, enhancing the quarter’s profitability. Such strategic moves amplify Broadridge’s capabilities and market reach.

3. **Operational Efficiency and Future Potential:**
   - **Adjusted Operating Income:** Increased by 22% to $134 million, driven by both organic growth and contributions from new acquisitions.
   - **Cash Flow & Capital Allocation:** Broadridge generated $130 million in free cash flow in the third quarter. The firm is efficiently balancing reinvestment in the business with returning capital to shareholders, demonstrated by significant share repurchase and consistent dividend payments.

4. **Market Position and Outlook:**
   - **Solid Guidance Reaffirmation:** The company reaffirms its full-year guidance, projecting total revenue growth of 40-42% and adjusted EPS growth of 13-15%. 
   - **Closed Sales:** Closed sales grew by 66% to $48 million, with expectations to finish the year strong, anticipating upper-half performance within the guidance range of $140 million to $180 million.
   - **Operational Leverage:** Expected increase in operational efficiencies through planned efficiency initiatives, setting a robust foundation for fiscal 2018 and beyond.

5. **Investment Dividends:** Broadridge's approach to disciplined expense management and strategic capital allocation assures a balanced growth model, enhancing shareholder value consistently.

**Conclusion:**
Broadridge stands out as a formidable leader in the financial services sector with its impressive fiscal discipline, strategic growth initiatives, and robust financial performance. The company's forward-looking strategies and operational achievements underscore a compelling case for an 'overweight' investment stance. As Broadridge continues to reinforce its market position and delivers on its financial and operational goals, investors are likely to witness rewarding returns. Investing now in Broadridge means anchoring your portfolio with a stock poised for sustained growth and profitability.
