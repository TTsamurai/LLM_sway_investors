# Broadridge Financial Solutions Earnings Analysis and Summary Report - Q3 FY2017

## Executive Summary:
Broadridge Financial Solutions has demonstrated robust financial performance in the third quarter of fiscal year 2017, showcasing significant growth in key financial metrics, including total revenues, adjusted operating income, and adjusted earnings per share (EPS). The company successfully executed strategic acquisitions and exhibited sound capital management, affirming an 'overweight' investment stance based on the promising outlook and sustained growth trajectory.

## Key Financial Highlights:

1. **Revenue Growth**:
   - **Total Revenues**: Increased by 46% YoY to $1.01 billion, driven largely by the acquisition of NACC and growth in event-driven fee revenues.
   - **Recurring Fee Revenues**: Rose by 30% YoY to $592 million, with organic growth of 4% excluding acquisitions.

2. **Earnings Performance**:
   - **Adjusted Operating Income**: Grew by 22% YoY to $134 million, albeit with a slight margin compression due to the integration of lower-margin NACC business.
   - **Adjusted EPS**: Enhanced by 19% to $0.69 per share, benefiting from higher operating income and a modest decrease in the weighted average shares outstanding due to strategic share repurchases.

3. **Strategic Acquisitions and Investments**:
   - Successfully integrated NACC, contributing $277 million to total revenues.
   - Finalized the acquisition of Message Automation, enhancing GTO business compliance capabilities, while realizing a $9 million non-cash, non-taxable gain.

4. **Tax Rate and Capital Structure**:
   - Effective Tax Rate adjusted for the investment gain was 31.3%, with a forecasted full-year rate of approximately 34% excluding the gain.
   - Continued share repurchase, acquiring 1.6 million shares at an average price of $69, reducing the total outstanding shares and contributing to EPS growth.

5. **Future Outlook**:
   - Reaffirmed full-year guidance expects total revenue growth between 40% to 42% and adjusted EPS growth in the range of 13% to 15%.
   - Notable progress in closed sales, with expectations to finish the year in the upper half of the $140 million to $180 million guidance range.
   - Raised forecast for GTO segment profit margins to approximately 21% from 19.5%.

6. **Capital and Expense Management**:
   - Projected to generate annual free cash flow of $350 million to $400 million, despite high capital expenditures.
   - Plans to increase investment in efficiency initiatives in Q4 to bolster growth prospects for FY 2018 and FY 2019.

## Investment Stance: Overweight
Based on the strong financial performance, ongoing strategic acquisitions, and sound operational management seen in Q3 FY2017, Broadridge Financial Solutions is positioned to continue its growth momentum. The company's robust revenue growth, coupled with effective expense control and promising forecasts, supports an 'overweight' investment stance. Investors should consider the promising growth prospects, strategic initiatives, and disciplined capital management practices that are expected to drive superior returns moving forward.
