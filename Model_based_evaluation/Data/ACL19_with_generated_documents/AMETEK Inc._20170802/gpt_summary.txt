The recent earnings conference call has provided a detailed summary of the financial and business highlights for AMETEK. The key financial metrics include:

1. Sales: Sales in the second quarter were a record $1.06 billion, up 9% compared to the same quarter in 2016. Organic sales were up 4%, acquisitions added 6%, and foreign currency was a 1% headwind in the quarter. The quarter also saw excellent growth in orders, with overall orders up 20% and organic orders up a very strong 12%.

2. Operating income: Operating income in the quarter was $232.4 million, up 6% from the second quarter of 2016. Reported operating income margins were 21.8% in the quarter with core margins at 22.7%, up 30 basis points versus the prior year.

3. Earnings per share: Diluted earnings per share was $0.65, an increase of 10% compared to last year's second quarter of $0.59 per share. The company has raised its full-year earnings guidance range to $2.46 to $2.52 per diluted share, up 7% to 10% over 2016 adjusted earnings.

4. Cash flow: The company's operating cash flow in the quarter was excellent, with free cash flow conversion at 123% of net income.

5. International sales: International sales in the second quarter made up 51% of total sales with very strong organic growth in China, India, the Middle East, and other parts of Southeast Asia, due to the investments made to expand the company's presence in these regions.

6. Acquisitions: AMETEK completed the acquisition of MOCON, a leading provider of laboratory and field gas analysis instrumentation. The company has deployed approximately $520 million in capital in 2017 and remains confident in its ability to continue to deploy free cash flow on value-enhancing acquisitions.

7. New product development: The company continues to invest in research and development, with the expectation to increase its investment in R&DE to approximately $220 million, representing about 5% of sales.

Overall, the company's financial and operating performance for the quarter was outstanding, with high-quality earnings growth driven by sales and superb operating performance. The company's optimism for the second half of the year is supported by solid orders, improved end-market conditions, and international growth opportunities. The guidance for the full year and third quarter reflects the company's confidence in its outlook. AMETEK's acquisition strategy, new product development efforts, and expansion in new markets are positioning the company for short- and long-term success.
