

Financial Analysis Summary:

1. Revenue growth: The company has demonstrated strong revenue growth of 9% in the third quarter of 2017, driven by diverse business opportunities and growth across customer segments and geographies.

2. Net income and earnings per share (EPS) growth: Net income grew 19% in the quarter, with EPS increasing by 25% from the third quarter of 2016. The lower tax rate and discrete charges had minimal impact on EPS.

3. Return on Equity (ROE): The company's ROE for the 12 months ending in September was 23%, slightly below historical performance but expected to trend back towards historical levels as the year ends.

4. Billed business performance: Worldwide FX adjusted billings grew 8% in the quarter, with consistent performance across different customer segments, particularly strong growth in SME and international consumer segments.

5. Loan performance: Loan growth accelerated to 14% on a reported basis and 13% on an FX adjusted basis. The shift in the loan portfolio has contributed to higher yields and slightly higher credit costs.

6. Expenses: Operating expenses were flat to the prior year, with a focus on cost reduction and ongoing progress on the initiatives to remove $1 billion from the company's cost base.

7. Effective tax rate: The effective tax rate for the quarter was lower at 26%, primarily due to the realization of certain foreign tax credits and an ongoing shift in the geographic mix of earnings.

8. Capital: The company has returned 97% of the capital generated thus far in 2017 to shareholders, leading to a 5% reduction in average shares outstanding versus the prior year.

Overall, the company has demonstrated steady and consistent financial performance, with an increase in full-year earnings per share guidance. The company is focused on delivering continued revenue and earnings growth while maintaining a strong capital position and profitability. Adopting an 'overweight' investment stance for the company based on the positive financial indicators and growth opportunities highlighted in the earnings conference call.
