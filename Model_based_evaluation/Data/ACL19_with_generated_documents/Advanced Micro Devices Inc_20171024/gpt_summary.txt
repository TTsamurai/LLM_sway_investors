Key Financial Metrics:

1. Revenue: 
   - Third quarter revenue of $1.64 billion, a 26% year-over-year increase and a 34% sequential increase.
   - Computing and Graphics segment revenue was $890 million, up 74% year-over-year.
   - Enterprise, Embedded and Semi-Custom segment revenue was $824 million, approximately flat year-over-year.

2. Gross Margin:
   - Gross margin was 35%, up 4 percentage points year-over-year.
   - Non-GAAP gross margin is expected to be approximately 35% for the fourth quarter of 2017.
   - It is expected to be greater than 36% in 2018.

3. Operating Income:
   - Operating income was $155 million in the third quarter of 2017, a solid improvement from $70 million a year ago.
   - Computing and Graphics segment operating income was $70 million compared to a loss of $66 million a year ago.
   - Enterprise, Embedded and Semi-Custom operating income was $84 million, down $52 million from a year ago, primarily due to higher costs.

4. Net Income:
   - Net income was $110 million or diluted earnings of $0.10 per share as compared to $27 million, or $0.03 per share a year ago.
   - Adjusted EBITDA was $191 million compared to $103 million a year ago.

5. Cash Position and Debt:
   - Cash, cash equivalents, and marketable securities total $879 million at the end of the quarter.
   - Long-term debt on the balance sheet was $1.36 billion.
   - Free cash flow was $32 million compared to $20 million in the year-ago period.

6. Expense Management:
   - Operating expenses were $419 million compared to $353 million a year ago, primarily due to higher R&D-related investments and expenses related to annual employee incentive programs.
   - Non-GAAP operating expenses are expected to be approximately $410 million for the fourth quarter of 2017.

7. IP Monetization:
   - Closed a patent licensing agreement that had a positive impact on both segments.
   - Several IP deals in the pipeline for the third quarter.

Overall, the company is reporting strong financial performance for the third quarter, with significant year-over-year revenue growth, improved gross margin, and higher net income. The company is also on track to achieve its long-term financial targets and is investing in R&D to support its product roadmap and go-to-market costs for new products. Additionally, the company expects revenue to increase by greater than 20% over 2016 and aims to manage expenses within the target model of 26% to 30% of revenue. The IP monetization efforts have also had a positive impact on the financial performance.
