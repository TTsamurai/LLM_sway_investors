These long-term investments are important for the community's future but may also impact short-term profitability.

4. Video Content and Community Building: The company is heavily investing in the growth of video content, with a focus on building community and facilitating meaningful social interaction. While the growth of video presents opportunities for revenue shares, the company acknowledges that not all types of content can be supported solely by ad models.

Overall, the earnings call transcript reveals a shift in focus towards long-term community building and security, which may impact short-term profitability and financial metrics, leading to a recommendation of an 'underweight' investment stance.
