Our financial performance for the September quarter reflects our achievement of $2.6 billion in revenue, and a record 70.3 exabytes of storage shipped, with an average capacity per drive of 1.9 terabytes. Our enterprise HDD market shipped 27.2 exabytes, with a record average capacity of 3.9 terabytes per drive. We also achieved over 1 million 10 terabyte units, up threefold from the June quarter, and sequential volume and revenue growth in our 12-terabyte helium nearline product. Our non-HDD revenue was 242 million, up 30% quarter-over-quarter and up 17% year-over-year.

Operating expenses for the September quarter were 481 million on a GAAP basis and 408 million on a non-GAAP basis, down 14% year-over-year. We divested the majority of our high-performance computing assets, resulting in a cost savings of approximately 20 million a year and impact revenues by approximately 50 million per year in our non-HDD revenue. We expect to exit the calendar year with non-GAAP operating expenses of approximately 400 million per quarter. Capital expenditures were 124 million for the September quarter, and we expect them to decrease sequentially for the December quarter.

Cash flow from operations in the September quarter was 237 million, with free cash flow at 113 million. We ended the September quarter with 2.3 billion in cash and cash equivalents, with 289 million ordinary shares outstanding. We have approved our quarterly dividend payment of $0.63 for the September quarter, payable on January 3, 2018.

Interest expense for the September quarter was 61 million, and we deployed 166 million to redeem 5 million shares and 22 million towards redeeming our 2018 senior notes. We have committed to provide up to $1.25 billion in financing to support the acquisition of Toshiba Memory Corporation, which we anticipate to be accretive to our earnings.

Overall, our operational and financial performance in the September quarter reflects execution of our business model and profitability improvement objectives. We continue to optimize our business and focus on aligning our go-to-market and product portfolio advancements towards the future growth opportunity markets. We are confident in our outlook and believe we are on track to achieve our financial goals for FY 2018.
