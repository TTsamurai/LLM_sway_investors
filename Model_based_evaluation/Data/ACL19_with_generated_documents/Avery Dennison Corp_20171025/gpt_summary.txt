There was a 25% increase in adjusted EPS, driven primarily by strong operating performance. Sales grew by 10% excluding currency, with 5.3% organic growth. Currency translation added about 1% to reported sales growth. The reduction in the tax rate also contributed to the improved EPS. Year-to-date free cash flow was up $8 million, and the company expects free cash flow conversion for the year to be nearly 100% of GAAP net income. The company's balance sheet remains strong, with capacity for investing in the business and returning cash to shareholders.

The Label and Graphic Materials (LGM) segment experienced 7% organic sales growth, with high value product lines growing faster than the base business. Operating margin for the segment was 13.1%, up 40 basis points versus the prior year. In the Retail Branding and Information Solutions (RBIS) segment, sales were up 7% organically, with RFID products up more than 25% in the quarter. Adjusted operating margins also expanded by 170 basis points to 8.7%. The Industrial and Healthcare Materials segment saw a 3.5% organic growth following the benefit of acquisitions, with the expectation of operating margin expanding to LGM's level or better in the coming years.

The company raised the midpoint of its guidance for adjusted earnings per share by $0.10 to an updated range of 490 to 495, reflecting strong underlying operating results. The company also expects reported sales growth of roughly 8% for the full year, with currency translation expected to be roughly neutral to sales and earnings. Incremental restructuring savings of approximately $50 million to $55 million are also anticipated for the year.

The company is experiencing some pockets of modest inflation in specific categories and regions, particularly in paper in Europe and Asia. Price increases are being implemented to address these inflation trends. The company continues to focus on material reengineering to reduce costs and adjust pricing as necessary. The company has also implemented targeted price increases in response to inflation in specific regions.

Overall, the company is pleased with its progress and remains confident in its ability to execute on its long-term strategic and financial objectives.
