Key Financial Metrics:

1. GAAP earnings for the quarter were $2.58 per share.
2. Incremental strategic investments of $178 million were made in the quarter, impacting earnings by $0.20 - $0.25 per share for the second half of the year.
3. Divestiture related activity added $0.57 to GAAP earnings per share, of which $0.54 relates to the identity management business.
4. Underlying earnings were $2.25 per share, up 8.2% year-on-year.
5. Quarterly sales increased by 1.9% in U.S. dollars, with organic growth of 3.5% and volumes up 3.8%.
6. Operating margins were 28% on a GAAP basis, or 24.3% year-over-year excluding the impact from incremental strategic investments and divestitures.
7. Second quarter free cash flow was $1.3 billion, up $378 million year-on-year, with free cash flow conversion at 85% and anticipated to be in the range of 95% to 100% for the full year.
8. Second quarter capital expenditures were $302 million, with a full year range expected to be $1.3 billion to $1.5 billion.
9. $701 million in cash dividends and $494 million through gross share repurchases were returned to shareholders during the quarter.
10. Second quarter operating income for Industrial, Safety and Graphics, Health Care, Electronics and Energy, and Consumer was $523 million, $852 million, $412 million, $301 million, and $195 million, respectively, with corresponding operating margins of 19.2%, 27.1%, 28.6%, 24.8%, and 17.2%, respectively.
11. Expectation of 50 basis points or more of year-on-year margin expansion in the second half of the year.
