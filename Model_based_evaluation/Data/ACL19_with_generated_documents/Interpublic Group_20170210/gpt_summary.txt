Based on the earnings conference call transcript, the key financial metrics can be summarized as follows:

1. Organic Growth: 
   - Fourth quarter organic growth was 5.3%
   - Full year organic growth was 5%
   - U.S. organic growth was 3.3% in Q4 and 4.7% for the full year, excluding the impact of lower pass-through revenue
   - International organic growth was 7.8% in Q4 and 5% for the full year

2. Operating Margin: 
   - Q4 Operating Margin was 21.4%, compared with 20.8% a year ago
   - Full-year operating margin expanded by 50 basis points to 12%

3. Diluted Earnings Per Share (EPS): 
   - Q4 diluted EPS was $0.78 and was $0.75 excluding expense of $0.06 per share for business dispositions
   - Full-year diluted EPS was $1.49 per share and would have been $1.37 per share excluding losses on dispositions and certain discrete items in the tax provision

4. Share Repurchases and Dividends: 
   - The company repurchased 13 million shares for $303 million during the year
   - The board increased the common share dividend by 20% to $0.18 per share quarterly and added $300 million to the share repurchase authorization

5. Revenue and Operating Expenses: 
   - Q4 revenues were $2.26 billion, with operating expenses increasing by 2.3% compared to reported revenue growth of 3.1%
   - Full-year operating expenses grew by 2.5% supporting revenue growth of 3.1%
   - Total salaries and related expenses to revenue for the full year was 64.2%

6. Tax Rates and Adjustments: 
   - The effective tax rate was low at 24.1% in Q4, adjusted for dispositions and discrete tax items, the adjusted effective tax rate was 31.2%
   - The normalized effective tax rate is expected to be in the range of 35% to 36% in 2017
   - Adjustments for business dispositions and tax provisions impacted diluted EPS

7. Cash Flow and Debt: 
   - Cash from operations in 2016 was $513 million, with a net decrease in cash and marketable securities of $409 million
   - Debt decreased from $2.3 million in 2007 to $1.69 billion at the end of 2016

8. Headcount and Operating Leverage: 
   - Year-end headcount was approximately 49,800, with an increase of 1.3% from a year ago
   - Operating leverage was achieved in various expense categories, including a 90 basis point leverage on O&G expense for the full year

9. Future Margins and Growth Targets: 
   - The company is targeting 50 basis points of margin for 2017
   - Margin target is not predicated on growth in a specific segment, and the focus is on gaining incremental efficiency in every dollar spent

In conclusion, the company demonstrated strong revenue growth, expense discipline, and balance sheet strength in the reported period. The focus on margin improvement and operational efficiency indicates a strong strategic direction for future growth and profitability.
