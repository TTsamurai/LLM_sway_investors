## Earnings Conference Call Summary for Q1 2017

### Financial Performance Overview

#### Quarter Highlights
- **Consolidated Revenue:** $2.3 billion, up 21.9% from the previous year.
  - Revenue from parts and services increased by 21.7%.
  - Other revenue rose by 25.9%.
- **Gross Margin:** Improved slightly by 10 basis points to 39.7%.
  - North American productivity gains offset declines in Europe and Specialty segments.
- **Operating Expenses:** Efficiency gains of about 10 basis points, primarily due to reduced facility and warehousing expenses.
- **EBITDA:** $290 million, a 23% increase from last year. EBITDA margin increased from 12.3% to 12.4%.
- **Operating Income:** Grew by approximately 27%.
- **Interest Expense:** Increased by $9.4 million due to higher borrowings related to acquisitions.
- **Pre-tax Income:** Increased to $213 million, up 28%.
- **Net Tax Rate:** Increased to 33.9% due to geographical mix of earnings.
- **EPS (Diluted):** Rose by 25% to $0.45. Adjusted EPS increased to $0.49, up by 17%.
- **Capital Allocation:** Strong focus on reducing debt with $326 million paid down, facilitated by the PGW glass manufacturing sale.

#### Segment Performance
- **North America:**
  - Revenue: $1.208 billion, an 11.8% increase. 
  - EBITDA: $176 million, up 20.9%. EBITDA margin improved to 14.6%.
  - Significant margin improvement due to effective procurement strategies and benign winter effects.
  - Organic growth was 1.8%, with additional growth from acquisitions and foreign exchange impacts.

- **Europe:**
  - Revenue: $821 million, a 50% increase. Organic growth at 8.5%.
  - EBITDA: $79 million, up 36.9%. EBITDA margin decreased to 9.6% due to lower gross margins and the inclusion of lower-margin businesses like Rhiag.
  - Adjusting for currency, EBITDA growth was 47.5%.

- **Specialty:**
  - Revenue: $315 million, up 6.7% with an organic growth rate of 6.3%.
  - EBITDA: $35 million, a 6% increase. EBITDA margin remained flat at 11.3%.

### Guidance for 2017
- **Organic Growth for Parts and Services:** Expected to be between 4.0% to 6.0%.
- **Adjusted EPS Range:** Increased to $1.82 to $1.92, potentially reaching a net income midpoint of $580 million.
- **Effective Tax Rate:** Projected at 35.25% before discrete items.
- **Cash Flow from Operations:** Guided to be in the range of $615 million to $645 million.
- **Capital Expenditures:** Estimated to remain between $200 million and $225 million.

### Strategic Focus
- Continued debt reduction with a solid focus on capital allocation to sustain growth.
- Enhancements in operational efficiency across segments, particularly in North America.
- Ongoing integration and leveraging of recent acquisitions to optimize overall performance and profitability.

---

This summary encapsulates the robust performance and strategic initiatives outlined during the Q1 2017 earnings call, highlighting a strong start to the year, sustained growth trajectory, and effective management of operational and financial metrics.
