Based on the recent earnings call transcript, the company has shown strong financial performance in the March 2017 quarter, with record levels of shipments, revenue, gross margin, operating income, and earnings per share. The balance sheet showed a solid cash position and healthy cash generation, with cash and short-term investments increasing to $6.140 billion. Cash from operations was $423 million, and deferred revenues stood at $842 million, up 37% from the prior quarter. The company also increased capital expenditures and headcount to support growing business levels.

Looking ahead, the company provided non-GAAP guidance for the June quarter, expecting another record shipment, record revenue, gross margin of 46%, operating margins of 27%, and earnings per share of $3. The company expressed confidence in their strong financial position and continued execution.
