### Earnings Conference Call Summary: Third Quarter 2017 Financial Results

#### Financial Performance Overview
- **Earnings Per Share (EPS)**: The company reported EPS of $2.46 for the third quarter of 2017, up from $2.35 in the same quarter of 2016.
- **Gross Margin**: Increased by $0.22 per share YOY, mainly due to:
  - A rate increase approved by the commission effective August 19, contributing an additional $0.13 per share.
  - Higher sales increasing earnings by $0.02 per share, attributed to customer growth, partly offset by energy efficiency initiatives and weather variations also adding $0.02 per share.
- **Operating Costs**:
  - Operations and maintenance expenses increased earnings impact by -$0.02 per share, primarily due to higher employee benefit costs.
  - Higher plant outage costs related to SCR installation at Four Corners unit 5.
  - Depreciation and amortization expenses also rose, impacting earnings by -$0.07 per share, driven by asset additions and an increase in D&A rates from the rate case.
  
#### Economic and Market Update: Arizona and Metro Phoenix
- **Job Growth**: Employment in Metro Phoenix grew by 2% through August, surpassing the national average of 1.5%.
- **Unemployment Rate**: The unemployment rate in Metro Phoenix stood at 4.3%.
- **Real Estate**: Both commercial and residential markets showed positive trends, with vacancy rates decreasing to levels last observed in 2008 or earlier. Approximately 3 million square feet of new office and retail construction was noted at the quarter's end.
- **Residential Market**: Continuation of growth in housing construction with single-family home permits at their highest since 2006.
