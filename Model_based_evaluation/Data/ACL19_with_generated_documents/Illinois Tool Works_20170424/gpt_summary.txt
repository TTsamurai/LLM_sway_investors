Summary:
- GAAP EPS increased 19% to $1.54, 21% excluding effects of currency translation. Organic growth was 3.5% in the quarter.
- Total revenue was $3.5 billion, an increase of 6%. Six of seven segments had positive organic growth and Welding improved to flat.
- Operating margin improved 120 basis points over last year, driven by enterprise strategy initiatives and volume leverage.
- Operating income grew 12% to $809 million, the highest quarterly total in the company's 105-year history.
- Free cash flow of $399 million was 74% of net income, but when adjusted for tax and pension items, free cash flow conversion would have been 91%.
- Automotive OEM segment delivered above plan organic revenue growth of 9%, with North America up 4% and Europe up 12%.
- Food Equipment segment was up 2% organically, with North America up 1% and international up 3%.
- Test & Measurement and Electronics segments both grew organic revenue by 6%, with strong demand in semiconductor-related end markets.
- Welding organic growth was flat in Q1, but demand improved 3% sequentially from Q4 to Q1. Operating margin performance was the highest in the company at 27.7%.
- Polymers & Fluids delivered another solid quarter of positive organic revenue growth of 2%, with operating margin of 20.6%.
- Construction Products was up 3% organically, with international growth strong in Europe (8%) and Asia Pacific (5%).
- Specialty Products organic revenue was up 1%, with operating margin at 26.9%.
- Full year earnings guidance was raised by $0.20 to a new EPS range of $6.20 to $6.40, with operating margin expected to exceed 23.5% for the full year.
- Second quarter EPS guidance is $1.55 to $1.65 with organic growth of 2% to 4%.

Overall, the company reported strong financial results for the first quarter of 2017, with increases in GAAP EPS, total revenue, operating income, and free cash flow. Segment performance varied, with Automotive OEM and Test & Measurement and Electronics segments showing strong organic revenue growth, while the Welding segment saw flat organic growth. The company raised its full year earnings guidance and expects higher organic growth and operating margin for the year.
