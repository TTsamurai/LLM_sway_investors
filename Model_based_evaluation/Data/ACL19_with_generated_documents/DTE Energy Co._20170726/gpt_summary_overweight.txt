On the financial front, the company is evaluating bids for the acquisition of existing generation plants and is planning to construct additional renewable energy capacity. DTE's carbon reduction plan is seen as a defining initiative for the company and the industry, with a focus on maintaining affordability while achieving sustainability goals.

It is important to note that while the FERC certificate for the NEXUS project has been delayed, the company remains confident in the project's progress and will provide a more precise projected in-service date once the certificate is received.
