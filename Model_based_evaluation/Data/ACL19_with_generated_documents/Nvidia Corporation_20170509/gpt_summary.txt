Key Financial Metrics from the recent earnings conference call transcripts:

1. Quarterly Revenue: The company reported a quarterly revenue of $1.94 billion, marking a 48% increase from the previous year.
2. Revenue Growth by Segments:
   - Gaming: Gaming revenue in the first quarter was $1.03 billion, up 49% year-on-year.
   - Professional Visualization: Quadro revenue grew to $205 million, up 8% from a year ago.
   - Data Center: Record revenue of $409 million was nearly triple that of a year ago.
   - Automotive: Revenue grew to a record $140 million, up 24% year over year and 9% sequentially, primarily from infotainment modules.
3. Gross Margin: GAAP and non-GAAP gross margins for the first quarter were 59.4% and 59.6% respectively. 
4. Operating Expenses: GAAP operating expenses were $596 million. Non-GAAP operating expenses were $517 million, up 17% from a year ago.
5. Operating Income: GAAP operating income was $554 million and non-GAAP operating income was $637 million, nearly doubling from a year ago.
6. Net Income: GAAP net income was $507 million. Non-GAAP net income was $533 million, more than doubling from a year ago.
7. Dividend to Shareholders: The company intends to return approximately $1.25 billion to shareholders through share repurchases and quarterly cash dividends.
8. Outlook for Second Quarter: The company expects revenue to be $1.95 billion plus or minus 2%, with expected growth of 3% sequentially.

These key financial metrics demonstrate the company's strong performance and growth across its various business segments, with a positive outlook for the future.
