Key financial metrics to focus on:
1. Revenue: KLA-Tencor reported a revenue of $914 million in the March quarter, above the midpoint of their guidance. They are expecting revenue to be in the range of $885 million to $945 million in the June quarter.
2. Earnings per share: GAAP diluted earnings per share was $1.61 in the March quarter and non-GAAP diluted earnings per share was $1.62.
3. New orders: New orders were $990 million in the March quarter, with foundry representing 54% of new system orders and memory representing 42% of new orders. They are modeling foundry orders to be approximately 50% of the total and memory orders to be about 40% of the total in the June quarter.
4. Shipments: KLA-Tencor reported total shipments of $909 million in the March quarter, finishing above the midpoint of their guidance. They are expecting June quarter shipments to be in a range of $890 million to $970 million, which would be a new record at the midpoint of guidance.
5. Gross margin: Non-GAAP gross margin was 62.5% in the March quarter, in line with expectations for the quarter. They are expecting gross margin to be in the range of 62.5% and 63.5% in the June quarter, up about 50 basis points at the midpoint versus the March quarter.
6. Operating expenses: Total non-GAAP operating expenses were $224 million in March, and they are modeling operating expense levels of between $234 million and $238 million in the June quarter.
7. Cash and investments: Cash and investments ended the quarter at $2.7 billion, an increase of approximately $111 million compared with the December quarter. Cash from operations was $225 million in the quarter and free cash flow was $215 million.
8. Guidance: KLA-Tencor's guidance for the June quarter is as follows: shipments in the range of $890 million to $970 million; revenue between $885 million and $945 million; and GAAP diluted EPS of $1.44 per share to $1.64 per share as well as non-GAAP diluted EPS of $1.46 per share to $1.66 per share.
