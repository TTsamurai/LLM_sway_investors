The company's focus on growth through increasing franchise ownership and improving same-store sales and net new unit growth is promising, but the transformation plan will take time to fully execute. The company's focus on delivering long-term, sustainable results is encouraging, but the challenges in the U.S. market may impact near-term performance. Therefore, it is advisable to adopt a cautious stance towards investing in Yum! Brands at this time.
