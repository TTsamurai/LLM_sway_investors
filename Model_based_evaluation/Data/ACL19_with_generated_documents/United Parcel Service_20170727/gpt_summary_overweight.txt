Key Financial Metrics:
- Total revenue increased 7.7% in the second quarter, with growth almost 9% when adjusted for currency.
- Earnings per share were $1.58, up almost 11% over last year.
- Revenue in the U.S. increased more than 8%, with total shipments increasing almost 5%.
- International revenue increased 2.8%, with adjust for currency growth at 8.3%.
- Supply Chain & Freight segment produced another solid quarter with revenue up 12% and reported operating profit improved by 24%.
- UPS ended the quarter with $4.6 billion in cash and marketable securities.
- Capital expenditures are $2 billion, putting UPS on track to lead the CapEx guidance range of 6% to 7% of revenue.
