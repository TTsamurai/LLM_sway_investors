Key financial metrics from the earnings conference call include:
1. Core FFO of $0.84 per share, including net promote income of $0.18 and core FFO, excluding promote income, of $0.66 per share
2. Leased almost 47 million square feet during the quarter with a 4% portfolio rolling in the second half of the year
3. Global occupancy at the end of the quarter was 96.2%
4. Share of net effective same-store NOI growth was 4.6%
5. Development starts were at approximately $900 million with margins over 20%
6. Completed $2.9 billion of financing activity with the majority denominated in sterling and yen
7. Leverage, following the USLF transaction, was approximately 25% on a market capitalization basis
8. Increased 2017 core FFO by $0.05 at the midpoint to a range between $2.78 and $2.82 per share, a 9% year-over-year increase
9. Cap rates modestly declining in the US and expected to decline in Europe and Japan
10. Expectations of compression in asset values due to rent growth
11. No special dividends expected and sheltering of expected gains from the USLF transaction
12. Yield perspective on cap rates for operating assets and land acquisitions
13. Same-store NOI expected to accelerate in 2018 due to wider gap in in-place to market rents
14. Anticipated slower leasing volume in the second half of the year as focus shifts to 2018 lease renewals.
