**Key Financial Metrics Summary from Recent Earnings Conference Call**

1. **Revenue Growth**
   - **Total Company Revenue Growth**: Increased by 13% year-over-year on an FX-neutral basis.
   - **Segment-specific Growth**:
     - Research Revenue: Grew by 15% on both reported and FX-neutral basis.
     - Events Revenue: Increased by 11% on an FX-neutral basis.
     - Consulting Revenue: Up by 2% on an FX-neutral basis from the previous year.

2. **Earnings Per Share (EPS)**
   - **Adjusted Diluted EPS**: Reported at $0.60 per share, aligning towards the upper end of the guidance range.

3. **Contract Value**
   - Total contract value grew by 15% year-over-year on an FX-neutral basis to $1.95 billion.

4. **Profitability Metrics**
   - **Adjusted EBITDA**: Showed growth of 1%, totaling $106 million for the quarter.
   - Gross Contribution Margin in Research: Declined slightly by one point to 69%.

5. **Client Retention and Wallet Retention**
   - Client retention stood at 83%, slightly down by 40 basis points year-over-year.
   - Wallet retention was reported at 104%.

6. **Free Cash Flow**
   - On a rolling four-quarter basis, free cash flow conversion was 126% of normalized net income.

7. **Debt and Financing**
   - Post-acquisition of CEB, gross debt increased by approximately $2.9 billion, totaling about $3.6 billion.
   - Net debt after closing was approximately $3 billion, translating to about 4.3 times leverage on a pro forma combined last 12 months of adjusted EBITDA.

8. **Guidance for 2017**
   - **Revenue**: Combined company adjusted revenue range for 2017 is between $3.4 billion to $3.5 billion.
   - **Adjusted EBITDA**: Expected to be between $685 million to $735 million for the year.
   - **Adjusted EPS**: Anticipated to be between $3.32 and $3.60 per share, representing a 5% to 7% accretion for 2017 from the acquisition of CEB.
   - **Capital Expenditures**: Estimated to be between $95 million to $105 million.
   - **Free Cash Flow**: Projected to range from $335 million to $365 million for 2017.

9. **Acquisition Impact**
   - The acquisition of CEB is expected to drive double-digit accretion to adjusted EPS in 2018.
   - Integration charges and acquisition-related adjustments were significant factors impacting the financial statements.

10. **Other Financial Aspects**
    - **SG&A Expense**: Increased by 18% year-over-year in Q1.
    - **Tax Rate**: GAAP tax rate for Q1 was 24.9%, with a normalized rate post-acquisition adjustments of 25.7%.
