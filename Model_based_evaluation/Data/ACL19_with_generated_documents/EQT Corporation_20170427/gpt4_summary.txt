**Summary Report on Recent Earnings Conference Call**

**EQT Corporation First Quarter Financial Highlights:**

1. **Acreage Acquisitions**: 
   - Two acquisitions completed, adding 67,400 core Marcellus acres. 
   - Total acquisition cost: $652 million, funded by cash on hand.
   - Increased total Marcellus core position by over 222,000 net acres since 2015.

2. **Pricing and Market Conditions**:
   - Average realized price: $3.50 per Mcfe, marking a 33% increase from $2.63/Mcfe in Q1 2016.
   - Average differential improved to -$0.13/Mcfe, representing a $0.54 improvement from Q4 2016.
   - Anticipated improvement in basis from -$0.25 in 2017 to -$0.13 in 2019 based on forward pricing.

3. **Financial Performance**:
   - Adjusted earnings per diluted share: $0.43 in Q1 2017, up from $0.05 in Q1 2016.
   - Adjusted operating cash flow: Increased to $322 million from $217 million in Q1 2016.
   - Net income attributable to non-controlling interests: $86.7 million in Q1 2017, compared to $82.7 million in Q1 2016.
   - Operating revenues: $828.7 million, a $345 million increase from Q1 2016.
   - Total operating expenses: $571.2 million, up 17%.

4. **Production and Operations**:
   - Production sales volume: 190 Bcfe, a 6% increase from Q1 2016.
   - Transmission expenses increased by $43.4 million due to higher volumes moved on pipelines.
   - Higher processing expenses by $16.7 million due to increased wet gas volumes.
   - Production taxes increased by $6.2 million due to better pricing.
   - SG&A expenses remained flat excluding a $5 million legal charge.
   - Lease operating expenses, excluding production taxes, decreased by $1.6 million.

5. **Midstream Performance**:
   - EQT Gathering income: $73.6 million, slightly up from Q1 2016.
   - EQT Transmission income: $71.5 million, an 11% increase from Q1 2016.

6. **Liquidity and Capital Expenditure**:
   - Zero net short-term debt with about $857 million in cash, excluding EQM.
   - Forecasted 2017 operating cash flow approximately $1.3 billion.
   - 2017 capital expenditure forecast at roughly $1.5 billion, to be funded by operating cash flow and on-hand cash.
   - Continued focus on maintaining an investment-grade balance sheet.

**Market and Future Prospectives**:
   - Continued use of increased transportation capacity to improve realized prices.
   - Expectation of significant volume increase and per unit cost reductions in H2 2017.
   - Strong positioning to fund future acquisitions while managing debt-to-equity ratios effectively.

**Conclusion**:
EQT's strategic acreage acquisitions and enhanced market positioning have contributed to a robust improvement in financial metrics. The company expects to sustain growth and operational efficiency, driven by improved pricing structures and strategic pipeline utilization. The financial strategy focuses on maintaining liquidity, leveraging operational cash flows, and sustaining an investment-grade balance sheet to support continuous growth and potential market opportunities.
