Operating expenses were up, reflecting negative impacts from the Sage product actions, weather-related disruptions, and the consolidation of the NOVADAQ acquisition. As a result, the adjusted operating margin was down 70 basis points from the prior-year quarter.

The company expects adjusted net earnings per diluted share, including dilution related to NOVADAQ and impacts related to Sage product actions and weather disruptions, to be in the range of $6.45 to $6.50 per share.
