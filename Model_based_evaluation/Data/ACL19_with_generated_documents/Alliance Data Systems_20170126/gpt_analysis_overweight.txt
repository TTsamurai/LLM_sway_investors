Overall, the company's performance in 2016 and its strategic measures to address challenges in certain segments indicate a favorable future outlook. The positive growth in revenue and adjusted EBITDA for LoyaltyOne and card services, along with the commitment to share repurchase, support an 'overweight' investment stance. Additionally, the potential growth drivers in BrandLoyalty and measures taken by Epsilon to improve competitiveness are further reasons to adopt an optimistic outlook for the company.
