2. **Stagnation in Operating Margins:**
   - Despite revenue growth, static operating margins indicate cost control issues or potential margin compression.

3. **Contribution from Acquisitions:**
   - Reliance on acquisitions for revenue boosts might not be sustainable long-term and could distract from necessary organic growth.

4. **Increased Operating Expenses:**
   - Slight increase in operating expenses, including those from acquisitions, which may not yet yield proportional benefits, indicating potential inefficiencies.

5. **Service Backlog Comprising Airwave Reduction:**
   - The increase in Services Backlog is padded by $400 million due to the Airwave backlog reduction, suggesting possible volatility or uncertainty in real service demand.

**Conclusion:**
Given these observations, maintaining an 'underweight' stance on the stock is recommended pending clearer indicators of efficient growth management and improvements in cash flow operations. Further monitoring of the impact of acquisitions and backlog realizations is advisable to reassess future investment positions.
