Key financial metrics from the recent earnings conference call transcripts include:

- Net sales of $3.8 billion in the fourth quarter, down 3% on a reported and organic basis, and full year net sales of $15.6 billion, down 6% as reported and 4% on an organic basis.

- Total segment operating profit of $673 million in the fourth quarter, up 4% in constant currency, and full year total segment operating profit of $3 billion, even, down 1% in constant currency.

- Net earnings increased 8% to $409 million in the fourth quarter, and diluted earnings per share were $0.69 as reported.

- Adjusted diluted EPS, which excludes certain items affecting comparability, was $0.73 in the fourth quarter, and $3.08 for the full year, up 6% in constant currency.

- Full year operating cash flow was $2.3 billion, down 12% from the year ago, and fiscal ‘17 capital investments totaled $684 million.

- Full year free cash flow was $1.6 billion, representing 86% of adjusted after-tax earnings.

- $1.1 billion in dividends were paid in fiscal ‘17, with dividends per share at $1.92, up 8% from last year.

- Net share repurchases totaled $1.5 billion, and average diluted shares outstanding were reduced by 2% for the full year.

- Fiscal ‘18 guidance includes expectations of organic net sales to decline between 1% and 2%, with total segment operating profits to be in the range between flat and up 1% on a constant currency basis.

- Full year adjusted diluted earnings per share are expected to be up between 1% and 2% in constant currency, with EPS down in the first half and up in the second half.
