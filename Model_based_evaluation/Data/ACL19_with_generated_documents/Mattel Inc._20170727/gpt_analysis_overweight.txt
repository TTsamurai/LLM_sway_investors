The company expects continued gross margin headwinds in the second half of the year but anticipates sequential margin improvement.

5. Strategic Investments: The company plans to continue strategic investments in emerging markets, incremental licensed entertainment portfolio, and key power brands in the second half of the year. However, they also expect continued declines in certain brands such as Monster High and Ever After High.

Despite challenges and headwinds, the company has a strong international presence, successful brands, and a focus on strategic investments. It is important to monitor how the company executes its strategies and manages its financial flexibility.
