Increased Focuses on Working Capital: The company has focused on improving working capital metrics to ensure efficient cash flow. They have worked with customers and suppliers to balance terms and improve collections, with particular attention on improving accounts receivable, accounts payable, and inventory processes. This has resulted in a very strong free cash flow for the company in 2016.

Capital Expenditures: The company has not limited capital expenditure and has allowed room for inventories to meet customer requirements, allowing some flexibility in working capital, and room for an approximate $250 million capital expenditure in 2017, primarily in productivity producing projects and precision machining in the factories.

Material Inflation: The company has experienced inflationary environment for steel in Q4 2016, and this trend is expected to continue in 2017. They are working towards getting the price over material inflation back to their norm.

Operating Costs and Corporate Expenses: Corporate costs for 2016 were under the typical run rate of $240 million and actually flat from 2015 to 2016 and going forward to 2017. The company is maintaining a tight control on costs in functional spending including IT, legal, HR, and finance.

Revenue Expectations and Market Outlook: The company expects total organic revenues to be up approximately 3% in 2017, with Climate segment showing good growth of approximately 4% organic and the Industrial segment expecting overall markets to be flat.

Free Cash Flow and Capital Allocation: The company plans to invest free cash flow into areas such as innovation, growth, and strategic investments, while also focusing on paying competitive dividends, share buybacks, acquisitions, and maintaining a strong balance sheet. The company expects strong free cash flow performance to continue in 2017, and a target of 100% of net income.

Earnings Per Share Guidance: The company expects continuing adjusted earnings per share for 2017 to be in the range of $4.30 to $4.50 excluding about $0.15 of restructuring.

Tax Rate and Operating Margins: The company expects its tax rate to remain between 21% and 22% for 2017 and adjusted operating margins for the Climate segment to be in the range of 14.5% to 15%, and in the range of 11% to 12% for the Industrial segment.
