Summary of Key Financial Metrics:

1. Record Q1 revenues of $1.7 billion and record digital revenues of $1.4 billion.
2. Growth in monthly active users (MAUs) for both Blizzard and Activision.
3. Strong pre-orders for Call of Duty: World War II and Destiny 2.
4. 40 billion hours of gameplay over the past 12 months.
5. Increase in total in-game purchases by almost 30% year-over-year, primarily driven by Overwatch and World of Warcraft.
6. Bookings per paying user rose for the 7th quarter in a row to a new record for King.
7. Continuation of successful mobile gaming projects and partnerships, including a Call of Duty mobile game.
