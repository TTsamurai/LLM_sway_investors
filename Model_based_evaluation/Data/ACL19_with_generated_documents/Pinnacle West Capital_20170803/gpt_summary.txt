Key Financial Metrics:
- Earnings per share: increased from $1.08 in Q2 2016 to $1.49 in Q2 2017
- Total gross margin: increased by $0.27 per share compared to Q2 2016
- Net sales: increased earnings by $0.10 per share due to improving economic conditions and customer growth
- Weather-normalized retail, kilowatt-hour sales: up 2.9% in quarterly comparison
- Operations and maintenance expense: lower, contributing $0.14 per share in Q2 2017
- Depreciation and amortization: decreased earnings by $0.01 per share in Q2 2017
- Employment in the Metro Phoenix area: grew by 2.4% compared to 1.6% for the entire U.S.
- Housing permits in Maricopa County: expected to increase by about 5,000 in 2017, driving growth in the residential real estate market
- APS's retail customer base: grew by 1.8% in the second quarter
- Financing and guidance plans: expected to issue about $650 million of additional long-term debt this year and provide earnings guidance for 2017 and 2018

These key financial metrics reflect a positive trend in earnings and sales growth, as well as the overall economic conditions in the Metro Phoenix area, supporting continued commercial and residential development. Additionally, the company's financing plans and guidance for the future indicate a strong balance sheet and liquidity.
