### Summary Report on Earnings Conference Call - Q4 and Full Year 2016 Financial Results

#### Overview:
During the recent earnings conference call for the fourth quarter and full year of 2016, key financial metrics and segment performances were reviewed alongside strategic corporate activities, including acquisitions and divestitures. The company reported organic revenue growth and margin expansion across various segments, beating both quarterly and annual expectations set from previous forecasts.

#### Key Financial Metrics for Q4 and FY 2016:
1. **Revenue Growth:**
   - **Q4:** Increased by 4.8% organically, with segment-specific growth.
   - **Full Year:** Grew by 4.6% organically.

2. **EBITDA:**
   - **Q4:** $846 million, rising 15.2% YOY.
   - **Full Year:** Reached $2.9 billion, up by 11.1% YOY.

3. **EBITDA Margin:**
   - Expanded by 220 basis points to 31.2%.

4. **Adjusted Net Earnings:**
   - **Q4:** From continuing operations was $377 million.
   - **EPS for Q4:** Increased 22.6% to $1.14 per share.
   - **EPS for Full Year:** Rose 18.6% to $3.82 per share.

5. **Free Cash Flow:**
   - **Q4:** $435 million, with a conversion rate of 115% to adjusted net earnings.
   - **Full Year:** $1.5 billion.

6. **Debt Reduction:**
   - Reduced by approximately $1 billion in 2016, standing at $10.5 billion as of December 31, 2016.

7. **Dividends and Share Repurchases:**
   - Returned $341 million in dividends.
   - Announced a 12% increase to quarterly dividends to $0.29 per share.

#### Segment Performance:
1. **IFS:**
   - Organic growth of 5% in FY 2016, with a slight decrease in EBITDA growth due to lower incentive accruals.
   
2. **GFS:**
   - Q4 revenue grew by 7.7% organically, with EBITDA up by 31.3%, reflecting a 700 basis point margin expansion.
   - Full year demonstrated a 5% revenue increase and a 17.2% rise in EBITDA.

3. **Banking and Payments:**
   - Growth was stable with specific increments in card processing volumes and payment strength observed across global markets.

4. **Corporate and Other:**
   - Reported a revenue of $159 million in Q4 with an EBITDA loss of $44 million, mainly due to increased corporate expenses.

#### Strategic Initiatives:
- Successfully closed the sale of the public sector and education businesses generating a significant influx of cash used to pay down debt.
- Anticipated debentures redemption further accelerates deleveraging efforts.
- Continued focus on integration and synergy extraction post the SunGard acquisition, targeting a synergy run rate above $275 million by the end of 2017.

### 2017 Outlook and Guidance:
- **Organic Revenue Growth:** Estimated between 2% to 3%.
- **Adjusted EBITDA:** Forecast between $3.04 billion to $3.12 billion.
- **Adjusted EPS:** Projected at $4.15 to $4.30, representing a 9% to 13% growth YOY.
- Expect a continued focus on debt reduction alongside strategic assessments for share buybacks in late 2017 and 2018.
- Effective tax rate anticipated to be around 32%, benefiting from efficient tax planning and growth in lower tax geographies.

### Summary and Future Path:
The company concludes 2016 on a strong note with robust financial performance and efficient execution across its strategic priorities. Looking forward, the emphasis remains on sustained organic growth, operational efficiency, and shareholder returns amidst high market competition and ongoing consolidations. The strategic divestiture and focused debt reduction underscore financial prudence in alignment with long-term corporate goals.
