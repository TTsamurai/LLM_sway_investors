The Asia Pacific region reported revenue growth of 9.6%, with adjusted operating income of $0.6 million and an increase of 260 basis points versus the prior year.

The company affirmed its full-year available cash flow guidance of $300 million to $320 million, net of the $50 million discretionary pension funding payment.

The company has been able to offset the commodity headwinds through pricing actions and anticipates continued pressure on the cost side in the coming quarters.

The company's full year outlook remains positive, with the expectation of organic revenue growth of 6% to 7%. The focus on new product development and channel initiatives is expected to drive continued growth and pricing improvements throughout the year. Additionally, the company remains committed to driving cash flow to be at 100% plus of net earnings, with opportunities for strategic investments through M&A or share repurchases.
