Based on the recent earnings conference call transcripts, the following key financial metrics and highlights were observed:

1. Shipments: Record shipments of $971 million in the June quarter, up 7% on a sequential basis and finishing above the guidance range.

2. Revenue: Revenue was $939 million in the June quarter, finishing at the upper end of the range of guidance, and it is expected to be in the range of $910 million to $970 million in the September quarter.

3. Earnings per Share: GAAP diluted earnings per share was $1.62 in the quarter, and non-GAAP diluted earnings per share was $1.64.

4. Operating Expenses: Total non-GAAP operating expenses were $238 million in June, and non-GAAP operating margin was 37.6%.

5. Gross Margin: Non-GAAP gross margin was 63% in June, and it is expected to be in the range of 62.5% to 63.5% in the September quarter.

6. Cash and Investments: Cash and investments ended the quarter at $3 billion, an increase of $313 million compared with the March quarter. Cash from operations was a record $463 million in June, and free cash flow was $452 million.

7. Tax Rate: Non-GAAP effective tax rate was 21% in the quarter, with an expectation of 20% in the September quarterly tax rate due to a non-recurring tax benefit expected in the quarter.

8. Business Outlook: The company provided guidance for the September quarter with shipments in the range of $945 million to $1.025 billion, revenue between $910 million and $970 million, and GAAP diluted EPS of $1.48 to $1.72 per share; as well as non-GAAP diluted EPS of $1.50 to $1.74 per share.

9. Future Expectations: The company anticipates continued growth, with expectations for more customers in more locations and more offerings for them, as well as broader customers and a shifting WFE mix towards a more memory-centric focus in the upcoming year. Additionally, the company expects new products to contribute to revenue in the second half of the year and sees no reason why KLA shouldn't grow in line with the market as the company moves into 2018.
